# Hi, I'm Andreas 👋
### Digital Governance, AI Systems, RegTech, LegalTech, Industrial Technology & Platform Engineering

I work at the intersection of governance, advanced software systems, industrial operations, and operational infrastructure.

Most of my work takes place in private environments. This profile is not intended as a public portfolio of deliverables, but as a concise representation of my technical orientation, domain focus, and engineering philosophy.

## Profile
My work is shaped by the meeting point of industrial production, digital governance, applied AI, and high-trust technical systems.

I am particularly interested in environments where software does not exist as an isolated product layer, but directly supports production processes, operational continuity, regulatory alignment, institutional control, and accountable decision-making.

This includes:
- software-supported industrial and production environments
- governance-native system design
- AI-supported decision and knowledge systems
- secure platform and infrastructure engineering
- compliance-aware automation
- legal, regulatory, and operational technology contexts

## Domain Focus
- industrial production environments and software-supported operations

## Philosophy
I value systems that are precise, durable, and institutionally credible.

Good engineering is not only about technical correctness.  
It is about building systems that can be operated, examined, governed, and trusted over time - especially in environments where failure, ambiguity, or non-compliance carry real consequences.
